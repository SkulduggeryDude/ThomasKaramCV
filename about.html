About Me


I am a 25 year old male from Hamilton. I am currently studying at Wintec, undertaking a Bachelor of Applied Information Technology. I hope to start a career in IT, specialising in cybersecurity or network engineering. After leaving a previous degree in 2019 I took time off study to work and wait out the covid pandemic. During that time, I went solo travelling in Japan, moved out of home, vaccinated 12,000 members of the community, became a Buddhist monk, and discovered new things about myself. Now I am ready to start studying again and build my life to succeed. I have a passion for technology, I am a natural problem solver and have a fascination of how things work. These skills have given me the confidence and drive to succeed in study and work. I currently live in Hamilton with three fun flatmates, a happy axolotl, and a yellow breaded dragon.
